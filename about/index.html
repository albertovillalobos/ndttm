Bio

Next Door to the Moon is a pop/rock band based out of Los Angeles, CA. Formed in 2014 by lead vocalist, guitarist and songwriter Connor Daniel, this band is ready to take on the world with their unique sound and invigorating energy.

After signing with C & A management in January of 2015, the band released their debut music video for their single, "Tell Me When It's Over" which is described by music reviewers as fun, comedic, and captivating. “The video is professional, will make you identify with one side or the other and will definitely crack you up at points, and have you shaking your head at others – you have GOT to go and watch it!” writes Musical Seduction. In addition, the song itself has been featured on the World Famous KROQ radio station in Los Angeles, which was received with much acclaim. The band currently has one album released to date titled, Invincible, which is available at most online retailers, including iTunes, Amazon, and Google Play, along with streaming services such as Spotify, Jango, Last.Fm and more.
